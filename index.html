Vision & mission

A technology company, engineering the Kingdom's next systems.

Salisco builds large-scale systems, mobile apps and applied AI — focused on two sectors that move Saudi Arabia: Automotive and Manpower (QHR).

Mission

To design and ship dependable software, mobile apps and AI products that make our two sectors — Automotive and Manpower / QHR — faster, smarter and more reliable.
